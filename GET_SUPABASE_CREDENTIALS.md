# How to Get Your Supabase Credentials

## Step-by-Step

1. **Go to Supabase Dashboard**
   - Visit: https://supabase.com/dashboard
   - Click on your project: **Watercooler V1**

2. **Open Settings**
   - Click the **⚙️ Settings** icon in the left sidebar
   - Click **API** in the settings menu

3. **Find These Two Values:**
   
   **Project URL:**
   - Look for "Project URL" or "URL"
   - It will look like: `https://jjgvsmuvkbhfkwcesrcw.supabase.co`
   - Copy this entire URL

   **anon public key:**
   - Look for "anon public" or "anon" key
   - It's a long string starting with `eyJ...`
   - Click the copy icon next to it
   - Copy the entire key

4. **Send Me Both Values**
   - Just paste them here and I'll add them to your .env file!
